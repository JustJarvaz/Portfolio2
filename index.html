- About Me

I developed an interest in web and app development during the quarantine. I saw this meme one day that was like: "If you didn't learn a new skill during the quarantine then, you wasted your time." That pushed me from then on to learn something new. Which led me to Thinkful, a coding boot camp that I felt stood out from the few that had interested me. Now starting wasn't easy at all. I had to completely rewire my brain, from the way my thought process worked to the habits I had developed over my whole life. But I was willing to grow beyond my comfort zone and learn new things. I was mostly excited to be in a new environment and build a team of dedicated developers like me. Before learning to code, I was an aspiring Musician/Songwriter from "The Live Music Capital Of The World!" aka Austin, Texas. Because of that, My networking skills are superb. I WILL GET YOUR INFORMATION! DON'T FIGHT IT! It also means I have that creative spark one will need in this field. It allows me to think outside the box, the gift wrap, AND the store from which it came.

I have experience working with: React.js, JavaScript, HTML5, CSS3, Node.js, Unit Testing, JSON, RESTful APIs, Git/GitHub, Command-Line, and npm. I am eager to begin working with a solid team of like-minded developers.
